Relax...and Draw.

Click and drag to draw.

Press 's' to save your drawing.

Press 'e' to clear it.

For this project, I made a drawing machine that's circles. The process of drawing an ens≈ç circle is a a meditative practice of letting go and letting your hand do the drawing. What I suggest the user should do is make sure their posture is good, inhale deeply, and as they exhale, they draw the circle. They simply draw while not worrying about how perfect it is. Of course, the user doesn't have to just draw circles. They can draw other things too, like a mountain or a river

I made this simple drawing machine as a way for us college students to relax and possibly reflect on the semester. The end of semester is fast approaching and finals are coming up. For some of us, we are hanging on the edge of our seats, wondering whether we'll pass a class or not. I want to offer a way to take our minds off of those worries, even if it's just temporary.
